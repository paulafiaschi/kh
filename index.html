Københavns Hockeyklub

A hockey family since 1904

Københavns Hockeyklub (KH), located in Copenhagen, Denmark, is proud to be the oldest field hockey club in Scandinavia.

Both indoor and outdoor, we play on the highest level in Denmark, and regularly participate in EuroHockey competitions abroad.

We are a very social and diverse club, and have a long standing tradition of welcoming foreign players to be part of the KH family. So if you are interested in playing with us, don’t hesitate to get in touch.
